AI, Robots, and US Labor Markets & Public Health

AI, Robots, and US Labor Markets & Public Health



My current and long standing research project at Southern Methodist University. I am exploring how automation and artifical intelligence, represented by intelligent industrial robots, affect labor market outcomes and opioid usage in American communities. Through econometric modeling, I've found that the introduction of these robots have negative effects on wages and employment and increases the usage of various opioids. Much of the analysis was completed in R and STATA.
